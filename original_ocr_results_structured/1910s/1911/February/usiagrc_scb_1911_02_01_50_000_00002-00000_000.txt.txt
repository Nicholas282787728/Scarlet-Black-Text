Chicago Theological Seminary
to Grinnell College

Dear Fellow Students:

The Seminary observed
yesterday as its Day of Prayer for schools
and colleges. The reports by our students
from their colleges and universities were
fine. Enthusiasm for Christian serVice is
big part in college spirit. In fact it is
increasing everywhere.

The great need today is for strong
devoted leaders to shape up and direct this
spirit, of the age. It is the opportunity of
a. life time. I was at. Dartmouth, my own Alma
Mater, for a. few hours, two weeks ago. and
had splendid personal talks with nine of the

boys about this chance.
I have accepted an invitation to be
at. your college Thursday, February 9. It
would be a pleasure to meet. as many as pos~
sible of you, imdividually and in groups, to
talk over this kind of leadership as a life—
work. If any of you want to make engage—
ments in advance, drop me a line.
Fraternally,

OZORA S. DAVIS,

1911 Chicago, 111.
